About



This web portal is to help students view their respective answer papers and the marks obtained for each. Students are provided with features to view the marks obtained for particular sections along with remarks stated by the examiner.




Teachers play the role of examining the answer papers written by the students with respect to this web portal. This website provides an easy mechanism with which papers can be corrected at ease by teachers at any location and time. Teachers can give their feedbacks and views on particular sections of the papers. This interface provides a stress-free easy access for correcting papers.




Analytics plays a huge role in our system. The students’ marks are uploaded onto the database server upon which dynamic implementations have put forward mechanisms of analysis to establish relations between grades and marks simplifying the understanding of each student’s marks. Pictorial representations of students’ marks are displayed in the form of graphs as well. We have given a pie chart as well as a bar graph to depict variations of students’ marks.
